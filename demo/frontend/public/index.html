Welcome to Music Maker, a music sequencer that lets you upload your own beats to the streamer!
This application is in early concept testing and is locked to this Twitch Channel.
NOTE: Only works in Chrome and Safari.

  • Play

  • Stop

  • Clear grid

  • Volume

  • Increase length of measure

  • Change octave

  • Upload beats

  • Clear row

    • Click on the word 'Percussion' and 'Melody' to switch between sequencers.

    Sine, Saw, Square, Triangle change the sound of the melody sequencer.
